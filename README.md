# Life in Weeks

A minimalist life visualization and journaling app that helps you reflect on the passage of time. Visualize your entire life as a grid of weeks, where each dot represents one week of your life.

## How to Use

1. **Enter Your Birth Date** - Tell the app when you were born
2. **Set Life Expectancy** - Choose your expected lifespan (default: 80 years)
3. **Explore Your Life** - Hover over weeks to see dates
4. **Add Memories** - Pause on a week for 0.5 seconds to open the date picker
5. **Journal** - Click specific dates to write what you want to remember

## Privacy

- All data is stored **locally on your device**
- No data is sent to any server
- Your memories are private and never leave your device
- Clear your browser data to reset
